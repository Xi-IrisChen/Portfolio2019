WORK ABOUT RESUME


I want to make technology more human, more natural.


​Imagine a world where efficiency and intelligence is everywhere. You don’t need to carry a phone, drive a car, or manually turn off the light… Everything can understand you and is tailored for you. You can access all the information, whenever, wherever you want...

This is my initial ubiquitous AI dream. But it’s not a dream anymore. It’s happening!

My undergraduate in computer science let me come across and fall in love with technologies. Although a lot are already technically feasible, it’s still far from daily life. So the past year have been the most life-changing one yet. Not a pure developer, I want to be a designer! I want to make technology more human, more natural.I want to infuse more efficiency and intelligence to day-to-day experiences.




My icon: infinity within the finity.


Constantly trying to push the boundaries to explore 'infinity within the finity' is who I am and who I want to be. Although I only finally decided to a designer one year before, WITH 101% LOVE, my pulse is racing every day, and I can feel my improvement, step by step. Following is what I'm working on right now:

- A ML&UX enthusiast: I started to summarize daily product that use machine learning, the ability and constraints of machine learning from last summer. Planning to publish my article to share the knowledge!
- A podcast fan: I love listen to high resolution or Google design podcast while walking on the street or eating dinner.
- A visual design lover: While designing font, logo or poster, I enjoy the moment immersing in the beauty of design. Browsing other’s work and iterating my baby gradually become my habit.
